Our Story

Crafted with
Generations of Skill

Based in Pakistan, we specialize in handmade Zari embroidery — the ancient art of weaving gold and silver metallic threads into fine fabric. Each dupatta and kameez is crafted by experienced artisans who have passed this tradition through generations.

We bring this heritage to the global market, offering international buyers a direct connection to authentic Pakistani craftsmanship at fair prices.

  • ✦
    Pure Zari Thread — Real gold & silver metallic threads sourced from trusted suppliers
  • ✦
    Skilled Artisans — Each piece hand-embroidered by master craftsmen with decades of experience
  • ✦
    Custom Designs — We accept custom orders with your preferred patterns, colors & fabric
  • ✦
    International Shipping — Carefully packed and shipped worldwide via reliable couriers
  • ✦
    Quality Guarantee — Each piece inspected before shipment, photos shared at every stage
